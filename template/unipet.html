Unipet

UNIPET is an NFT game developed by Nextgen Lab that integrates blockchain technology to deliver a unique gameplay experience. In it, players will become leaders specializing in collecting and upgrading Unipets, creatures with irregular shapes and special abilities.

Unipets are divided into 2 types, Normal and NFT. When choosing Unipet, players need to consider the following points:
>> Quality
>> Fate
>> Evolution
>> Skill
Focus on gameplay, create attraction and pets with users.
Create a desire to own a pet and develop additional services to maximize revenue from non-blockchain services.
Develop Blockchain utility services such as NFT pet and free NFT transactions to have 1 more payment channel & 1 transaction property.
The game's direction becomes a Social Game, connecting players, applying the utility of blockchain but not depending on it.
